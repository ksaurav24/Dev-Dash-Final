WELCOME TO DEV-DASH MINI-HACKATHON

What is Dev-Dash?

Dev-Dash is a mini hackathon organized by Codement, a community of coders at NCER campus. It is a four-day event where you can showcase your skills in AI, Codement website contribution, or Education themes. You will get a chance to learn new technologies, network with mentors and peers, and win exciting prizes. Choose your theme, define your project and Dash to development.

  1. AI INTEGRATION:
    Use power of AI to create smart and innovative solutions for various domains and applications.
  2. CODEMENT CONTRIBUTION:
    Enhance and improve the Codement community website by adding new features and functionalities.
  3. EDUCATION:
    This theme is about using technology to enhance and facilitate learning and teaching.

Event Schedule

The Dev-Dash mini hackathon will be carried out on following timeline.

  • On 22-FEB(Thursday):
    There will be inauguration and a Git-Hub mentor session at NCER hall
  • During 22-FEB to 25-FEB(Thur-Sun):
    Hackathon on Devpost (Submission closes on Sunday Midnight)
  • On 26-FEB(Monday):
    Evaluation of the projects (you relax)
  • On 27-FEB(Tuesday):
    Announcement of results (chance to Network with alumni)

Prizes

Here are the amazing prizes that will be awarded to the top teams at the Dev-Dash hackathon:

  • 1st place: ₹4000 Prize money
  • 2nd place: ₹2000 prize money
  • In addition to the prize money you will also get Goodies and stickers From Codement
